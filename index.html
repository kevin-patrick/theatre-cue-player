Product Highlights
BETA RELEASE

The complete educational audio solution.
Professional-grade show control designed specifically for student operators and Chromebook environments.

Show Control

  • Unified Workflow: Fade All, Stop All, and instant "Go" playback.
  • Multi-Layer Playback: Seamless overlapping audio instances.
  • Automation: Auto-Follow, Auto-Continue, and Infinite Loops.
  • Hotkeys: Instant-fire sound effects grid (1-0).

Management

  • Export Center: Generate printable PDF Cue Sheets and portable Zip packages.
  • Waveform Editor: Visual non-destructive trimming and fading.
  • Offline First: Runs locally without internet (perfect for air-gapped booths).
  • Student Safe: "Help Mode" overlay preventing accidental edits.

Roadmap

  • Current Version: Stability and Core Audio Features.
  • Future Development: A dedicated PWA with Google Drive Integration, Video Playback, and MIDI/OSC Support.
